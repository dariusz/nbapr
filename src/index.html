Trade Deadline Pre-Season

Warriors unanimous, 76ers a mystery

Dariusz Grabka, July 31, 2017

Power rankings appeared following the trade deadline, confirming what you already knew: the Golden State Warriors somehow got better, claiming top spot on everyone's list.

GSW, HOU: One-two punch

Despite power rank heroics from the Houston Rockets, who moved up to number 2, the Warriors asserted their position as the consensus top team in the NBA. There was zero rank deviation; no-one dared question the dominance of Golden State's post-post-season re-up.

Trust the Rankings: No clarity for Philly

The Philadelphia 76ers had the largest deviation on this month's power rankings: 3.4 spots. Writers consider Philly a lock for anywhere from 15th to 22nd .. representing a spread of "playoff bound" to "walking dead" after the All-Star Game.

Celtics break-out in the East Rankings

What is this?
During the NBA season, teams are ranked weekly into "power rankings" by professional basketball writers. We aggregate those rankings, average them, and use standard deviation as a tie-break.
